From the previous Prototype Scenario 2-Length and 3-Repetition were selected, unfirming the total duration on 10 seconds. Two different version based on different kind of sound, if musical or not (noise) were created for each of those. These 4 final Scenarios were tested in an Experiment with real users who dedicated a day for each Scenario. During that day, they listened 8 Sonifications, which came every hour describing the status of system. Here we have collected the Sonifications they listened during the test. Sonifications are grouped per Scenario and hour they were played. Each Scenario is followed by the key-sound we provided to the user to identify the districts, during the test.
