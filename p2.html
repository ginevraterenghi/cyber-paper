This Prototype includes 4 different Scenarios based on different sound structure. In each Scenario, each sound represents a district wich gathers data based on components geographic position (map).
Scenarios 1, 2 and 3 base district identification on different sound and the anomaly level on a particular sound characteristic they are built on. In scenario 4, district are recognizible for the order they are played, while the pitch of each notes represent the level of anomaly.
Here, we have collected the Sonifications for a regular status, status with low and high level of anomaly. Each Scenario repeats the Sonification two times.
